Title IX x UMD

UMD has a long history history with Title IX. Bernice "Bunny" Sandler, UMD alumna, is credited as the "God Mother" of Title IX. In 1969, Bunny experienced predujice in the hiring process at the very school she was an alummus from. Sparked by her experience at the University of Maryland, she aided Representative Patsy Mink and Senator Birch Bayh to birth the greatest change to modern collegiate equality.

Secretary of Education, Betsy DeVos, has recently suggested changes to the way in which public universities are required to handle Title IX procedures. These changes will impact all who attend public universities, such as our very own University of Maryland.
