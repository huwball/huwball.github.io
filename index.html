New Title IX Proposal

Secretary of Education, Betsy DeVos, has recently suggested changes to the way in which public universities are required to handle Title IX procedures. These changes will impact all who attend public universities, such as our very own University of Maryland. See below to learn more about how these changes will impact your every day life at UMD and more on the history of Title IX.

Title IX at UMD

UMD has a long history with Title IX. Bernice "Bunny" Sandler, UMD alumna, is credited as the "God Mother" of Title IX. In 1969, Bunny experienced predujice in the hiring process at the very school she was an alummus from. Sparked by her experience at the University of Maryland, she aided Representative Patsy Mink and Senator Birch Bayh to birth the greatest change to modern collegiate equality.
